Who are us?

We are a group of Computer Science and Health Information Science at University of Victoria. Our goal is implement new tools and improving available tools to improve the experience of current and future students.

Our Client

The University of Victoria is one of the largest post-secondary institutions in British Columbia, with over twenty thousand undergraduate and graduate students enrolled. In such a large university like this, it can be challenging for students to find study spaces on campus, especially with how complicated the university has made it; with its current set of systems being spread across different websites. However, with a dedicated website for booking study spaces like StudyUp where everything is centralized and filterable, the process could be streamlined considerably and made easier.

Project

StudyUp’s goal is to unify UVic’s existing study space booking systems under one centralized system that can be both filterable and easy to use for students.
